Name of the company: Skite Solar

Skite Solar occupation: Skite Solar produces space-grade photovoltaics.

Description of the company: Skite Solar is a space technology company developing next-generation solar cells to address a critical bottleneck in satellite manufacturing. While the vast majority of spacecraft rely on solar power, access to space-grade solar cells is constrained by long lead times, high prices, and limited supplier diversity. These challenges disproportionately affect NewSpace and fast-growing satellite manufacturers. Skite Solar is leveraging perovskite photovoltaic technology to deliver higher performance, improved power-to-weight, and a more scalable manufacturing approach, while remaining compatible with existing solar array architectures.
